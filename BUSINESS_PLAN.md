## 1. Company Overview

### 1.1 Vision

FlowForge aims to become the default platform for workflow automation among small and mid-size businesses, enabling them to operate at enterprise scale without enterprise overhead.

### 1.2 Mission

To connect tools, eliminate manual work, and help teams focus on high-value tasks by providing reliable, accessible workflow automation.

### 1.3 Business Idea

FlowForge is a B2B SaaS workflow automation platform. We offer an integration hub, visual workflow builder, and managed automation services so companies can connect CRM, support, billing, and productivity tools without custom development.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Small and mid-size companies rely on multiple tools (e.g., Salesforce, Zendesk, Slack, QuickBooks) that do not integrate well. Data is copied manually between systems, causing:

- **Time loss** — Teams spend hours on repetitive copy-paste and status updates
- **Errors** — Manual data entry leads to mistakes, missed follow-ups, and poor reporting
- **Friction** — Poor integration slows onboarding, support, and sales processes
- **Scaling limits** — Manual processes do not scale as companies grow

Most workflow automation tools are aimed at enterprises or require technical staff. SMBs lack budget and in-house expertise for complex implementations. FlowForge addresses this gap by offering simple, configurable automation without custom code.

---

## 3. Products and Services

### 3.1 Workflow Automation Platform

Visual workflow builder that lets users define triggers, conditions, and actions. Integrates with 500+ apps. No coding required.

### 3.2 Integration Hub

Central connector layer for CRM, support, billing, HR, and productivity tools. Pre-built connectors for popular apps plus REST API for custom integrations.

### 3.3 Process Optimization

Process audit and design services. Our team maps current workflows, identifies bottlenecks, and proposes automation opportunities with ROI estimates.

### 3.4 Managed Automation

White-glove design, deployment, and maintenance of workflows. Includes monitoring, incident response, and quarterly reviews.

## 5. Business Model and Revenue Strategy

### 5.1 Model

- **Subscription SaaS** — Tiered monthly/annual plans by usage and features
- **Services revenue** — Consulting and managed automation

### 5.2 Revenue Streams

1. **Platform subscription** — Base + usage-based pricing (automations, integrations, tasks)
2. **Professional services** — Process optimization and workflow design
3. **Managed automation** — Monthly fee per workflow or per user
